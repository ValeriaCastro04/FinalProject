‚ÅâÔ∏è How can I change my major?

Changing your major can be a bit confusing since there is not a guide to do it. If you want to switch to a different major, you first need to speak with the dean of your current program and request the change. After that, you should meet with the dean of the new program you wish to join and explain your reasons for switching. If approved, the dean will process the change in the academic records system. You should see the update on the academic registration page within a few days or up to a week. If the change does not appear, contact the academic records office to confirm the update and ask for assistance. Sometimes, updates are visible only to faculty before they appear for students.

‚ÅâÔ∏è How do I withdraw from a course?

You can withdraw from a course only during the first three weeks of the trimester. To do so, you need to send an email to your dean or schedule a meeting to explain your reasons for withdrawing. The dean will then process the withdrawal directly in the academic system.
