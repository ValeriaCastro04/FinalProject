💻 How to Use the Virtual Platforms

ESEN uses two main online platforms that you'll be using a lot. Here's a quick breakdown so you don’t get lost:

Moodle (📚)

This is where your classes live online. Teachers upload slides, videos, homework, and quizzes here. You can submit assignments and check feedback as well.

🔗 Access Moodle

Academic Records System (🧾)

This is more administrative. You’ll see your grades, your academic transcript (CUM), and make official requests here. From second year on, you'll also enroll in your classes using this platform.

You’ll also use it to evaluate your professors and apply for assistant positions in certain courses.

🔗 Access Academic Records

To access both, you’ll need login credentials that the university provides. If you haven't received them yet, ask your coordinator or the help desk.

📚 Study Rooms at the Knowledge Center

Planning a study session with friends? ESEN's Knowledge Center offers reservable rooms you can check online. You’ll see what rooms are already booked and for how long.

  • 👥 Small rooms require at least 3 people to use them.
  • 👨‍👩‍👧‍👦 Got a big group (8+)? Ask for the large study room.
  • 🧽 Each room has a whiteboard — get markers and erasers at the front desk.
  • 🚫 No food allowed in the rooms.
  • 🔇 Please keep your voice down — other groups are working too!

Booking a room means more focus and fewer distractions. Plan ahead and secure your spot!
