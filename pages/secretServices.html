👨‍🏫 Become a Class Assistant or Tutor

Helping others = helping yourself! ESEN gives you two cool ways to support your classmates:


🔹 Class Assistant


Apply through the Academic Records system before the semester begins.

You’ll qualify if you:

  • ✔️ Got 8.5 or higher in the course
  • ✔️ Have a helpful attitude and time to support grading or extra sessions
  • ❌ Haven’t failed more than two classes total

🔹 Private Tutor


Want to be a 1-on-1 tutor? Contact Roxana Palacios, the tutoring coordinator. You'll have weekly meetings and report on your student’s progress.

Why become a tutor or assistant?

  • 💰 Already completed your social/eco hours? You’ll get PAID.
  • 🌱 Still working on them? You’ll earn 125 hours per subject.
  • ⚠️ Max: 2 assistantships + maybe 1 tutoring role (with special permission)

🧠 Counseling Services

Feeling overwhelmed? Need someone to talk to about mental health, time management, financial struggles, or just life in general?


ESEN’s Student Counseling Department is full of kind, professional people who truly care.

  • 🕘 Available: 9:00 AM – 4:00 PM
  • 📅 Flexible schedules: You can meet online or outside regular hours if needed!

Don't be shy — reaching out is a strong move. 💪
